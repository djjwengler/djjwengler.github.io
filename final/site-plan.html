Overview

Site Purpose

The Chamber of Commerce in Yardley, PA is dedicated to supporting and promoting the businesses within the city. This website will allow current members, future members, and citizens of Yardley access to the businesses available in Yardley, as well as information on how to join.

Target Audience

The ideal audience of this website will be members of the community looking to connect with other business owners, promote their own business, or find local sources for their needs.

Personas

First Persona
  • Fictional Name: Mollie Bennett
  • Job title: Stay at home mom
  • Demographics:
    • Age: 33 years old
    • Family: Married with 3 children
    • Income: $50,000 - $75,000
    • Education: Bachelor's or Associates
  • Goals: To support local businesses by buying groceries, gardening supplies, or other household needs from businesses in her area.
  • Environment: She is comfortable with computers, uses a desktop computer occasionally with high speed modem, but most likely will be accessing information via her smartphone.
Second Persona
  • Fictional Name: Julie Quinn
  • Job title: Bookstore Owner
  • Demographics:
    • Age: 44 years old
    • Family: Married with 2 children
    • Income: $75,000 - $100,000
    • Education: MBA
  • Goals: To promote her business to local citizens as well as other local businesses. She believes the community businesses are stronger when they help and support each other.
  • Environment: She is very comfortable with technology, uses a laptop at work and at home to plan HR activities, occasionally uses her smartphone during her personal time.

Typography

Heading Font: Oswald

This font is a contemporary san-serif that helps the website maintain a modern feel. Used primarily for headings and navigation.

Paragraph Font: Cardo

The serif font creates a nice contrast to the san-serif headline. This font is used for normal text found in paragraphs, lists, and forms.

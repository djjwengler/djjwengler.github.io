Personas

First Persona

  • Fictional Name: Mollie Bennett
  • Job title: Stay at home mom
  • Demographics:
    • Age: 33 years old
    • Family: Married with 3 children
    • Income: $50,000 - $75,000
    • Education: Bachelor's or Associates
  • Goals: To plan family activities, create memories, and get everyone out of the house (weather dependent)
  • Environment: She is comfortable with computers, uses a desktop computer occasionally with high speed modem, but most likely will be accessing information via her smartphone.

Second Persona

  • Fictional Name: Julie Quinn
  • Job title: HR Director
  • Demographics:
    • Age: 44 years old
    • Family: Married with 2 children
    • Income: $75,000 - $100,000
    • Education: Masters degree
  • Goals: To plan work and HR events, to adjust for weather conditions when company team building activities are outside.
  • Environment: She is very comfortable with technology, uses a laptop at work and at home to plan HR activities, occasionally uses her smartphone during her personal time.

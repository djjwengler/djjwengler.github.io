    Fish Haven Town Events

    Pizza in the Park!

    Fish Haven Pizza in the park!! Come join the town every Saturday afternoon at 12:00 in the city park pavilion, organized by the Fish Haven Parks and Rec Department. The town supplies all you need - plate, fork, pizza! In the past we've had as many as 207 town citizens join us for this decades long tradition. Bring blankets or lawn chairs and something to drink! Cold sodas or thermoses of lemonade are popular additions. The pizza (one slice per citizen!) is provided at no cost - to those who want to contribute, the Parks and Rec department accepts donations to support this cherished town treasure. Choose from common pizza such as plain cheese, pepperoni, veggie, and the Fish Haven Special - fish!

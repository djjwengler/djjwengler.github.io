    Pancakes in the Park!

    Preston Pancakes in the park!! Come join the town every Saturday morning at 9:00 in the city park pavilion, organized by the Preston Parks and Rec Department. The town supplies all you need - plate, fork, pancakes, sausages, and plenty of maple syrup! In the past we've had as many as 236 town citizens join us for this decades long tradition. Bring blankets or lawn chairs and something to drink! Thermoses of hot chocolate or orange juice are popular additions as well. The pancakes are provided at no cost - to those who want to contribute, the Parks and Rec department accepts donations to support this cherished town treasure.

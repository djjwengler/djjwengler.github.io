    Soda Springs Town Events

    Picnic in the Park!

    Soda Springs Picnic in the park!! Come join the town every Friday evening at 5:00 in the city park pavilion, organized by the Soda Springs Parks and Rec Department. Bring your own picnic - don't forget the paper plates! In the past we've had as many as 337 town citizens join us for this decades long tradition. Bring blankets or lawn chairs and remember the town provides cold sodas and lemonade. At 7pm sharp during the summer months stay a little longer and watch a movie in the park! Selections are always rated G or PG. If you would like to suggest a movie for all of us to enjoy, contact the Parks and Rec department.
